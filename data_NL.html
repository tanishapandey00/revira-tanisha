AnalyzeSentiment(text)

Inspects the given text and identifies the prevailing emotional opinion within the text, especially to determine a writer's attitude as positive, negative, or neutral

EXAMPLES

AnalyzeSentiment(“Enjoy your vacation”) = 1 [Positive statement]

INPUTS

text : A text value

OUTPUTS

Outputs a sentiment json with values ‘magnitude’ and ‘score’. We use score value for our analyses
